Set the scene...

Cosy blankets, sweaters and wooly socks is all you need. Lighting some candles will make you feel even more cosy. You decide what makes you feel good! Remember, it's not about going to Ikea to get the candle, but appreciating the twinkly light and being mindful is what counts. It's about feeling content in a safe place, whilst it's raining outside, rather than feeling blue because it's raining.

Enjoy it your way...

It's about spending time with your loved ones or enjoying your own company if you need some "me time". You can grab a book, have a long relaxing bath, play board games, cook a meal or do some yoga, and there is nothing more hygge than ending a long winter walk with a hot cup of cocoa. Whatever you do, don't forget it's the little things in life that matter the most!
